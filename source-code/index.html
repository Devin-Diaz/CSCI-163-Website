AI in CS Education

This website is based on a semester-long project in the CSCI-163 Discovering Artificial Intelligence: Applications, Ethics, and Beyond course.

Objectives: Educating a lay audience on the exploration regarding the impact of artificial intelligence on computer science education, student learning, and career prospects.
